
### Log and Ln rules

$$
\begin{array}{c@{\qquad}c}
\textbf{Logarithm Rules (base 10)} & \textbf{Natural Log Rules} \\[8pt]

\log(xy) = \log(x) + \log(y)
& \ln(xy) = \ln(x) + \ln(y) \\[6pt]

\log\!\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = \log(x) - \log(y)
& \ln\!\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = \ln(x) - \ln(y) \\[6pt]

\log(x^a) = a\log(x)
& \ln(x^a) = a\ln(x) \\[6pt]

\log(10^x) = x
& \ln(e^x) = x \\[6pt]

10^{\log(x)} = x
& e^{\ln(x)} = x
\end{array}
$$
