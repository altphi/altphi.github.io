
$$
\begin{array}{c@{\qquad}c}
\textbf{Logarithm Identities} & \textbf{Natural Log Identities} \\[8pt]

\log(xy) = \log(x) + \log(y)
& \ln(xy) = \ln(x) + \ln(y) \\[6pt]

\log\!\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = \log(x) - \log(y)
& \ln\!\left(\frac{x}{y}\right) = \ln(x) - \ln(y) \\[6pt]

\log(x^a) = a\log(x)
& \ln(x^a) = a\ln(x) \\[6pt]

\log(10^x) = x
& \ln(e^x) = x \\[6pt]

10^{\log(x)} = x
& e^{\ln(x)} = x
\end{array}
$$


### Derivatives

$$\frac{d}{dx} \log_a(x) = \frac{1}{x \ln(a)}$$

$$\frac{d}{dx} \log_a(f(x)) = \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)\ln(a)}$$

$$\frac{d}{dx} \ln(x) = \frac{1}{x}$$

$$\frac{d}{dx} \ln|x| = \frac{1}{x}$$

$$\frac{d}{dx} \ln(f(x)) = \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)}$$

### Integrals

$$\int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln|x| + C$$

$$\int \frac{f'(x)}{f(x)} \, dx = \ln|f(x)| + C$$
