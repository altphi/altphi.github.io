
### A longer list of derivatives

(MathAcademy is having me review a lot of calc lately.)

$$
\begin{array}{c|c}
\text{Function / Rule} & \text{Derivative} \\
\hline
c & 0 \\
x & 1 \\
cx & c \\
x^n & nx^{n-1} \\
c f(x) & c f'(x) \\
f(x) + g(x) & f'(x) + g'(x) \\
f(x) - g(x) & f'(x) - g'(x) \\
f(x)g(x) & f'(x)g(x) + f(x)g'(x) \\
\dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)} &
\dfrac{f'(x)g(x) - f(x)g'(x)}{g(x)^2} \\
f(g(x)) & f'(g(x))g'(x) \\
e^x & e^x \\
e^u & e^u u' \\
a^x & a^x \ln a \\
a^u & a^u \ln(a) \cdot u' \\
\ln x & \dfrac{1}{x} \\
\ln u & \dfrac{u'}{u} \\
\log_a x & \dfrac{1}{x\ln a} \\
\sin x & \cos x \\
\cos x & -\sin x \\
\tan x & \sec^2 x \\
\sec x & \sec x \tan x \\
\csc x & -\csc x \cot x \\
\cot x & -\csc^2 x \\
\sin u & \cos u \cdot u' \\
\cos u & -\sin u \cdot u' \\
\tan u & \sec^2 u \cdot u' \\
\arcsin x & \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \\
\arccos x & -\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}} \\
\arctan x & \dfrac{1}{1+x^2}
\end{array}
$$
