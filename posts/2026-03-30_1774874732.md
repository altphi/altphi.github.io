
## combinatorics formulae

$$
\begin{array}{|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
\textbf{Case} & \textbf{Notation} & \textbf{Order Matters} & \textbf{Repetition Allowed} & \textbf{Formula} \\
\hline
\text{Permutation (no repetition)} & {}_{n}P_{k} & \text{Yes} & \text{No} & \dfrac{n!}{(n-k)!} \\
\hline
\text{Permutation (with repetition)} & \text{---} & \text{Yes} & \text{Yes} & n^k \\
\hline
\text{Combination (no repetition)} & {}_{n}C_{k} \ (\text{or } \binom{n}{k}) & \text{No} & \text{No} & \dfrac{n!}{k!(n-k)!} \\
\hline
\text{Combination (with repetition)} & \text{---} & \text{No} & \text{Yes} & \binom{n+k-1}{k} \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$
