
> ‘Gigabyte’ can mean two things: 10^9, or 2^30. The IEC standard resolved this
> by stating that ‘gigabyte’ is 10^9, and ‘gibibyte’ is 2^30. However, very few
> people use this terminology, and rely on context to differentiate.
