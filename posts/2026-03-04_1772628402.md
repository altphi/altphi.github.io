
Counterclockwise Rotations
- 90°   : $$(x, y) \rightarrow (-y,  x)$$
- 180°  : $$(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, -y)$$
- 270°  : $$(x, y) \rightarrow (y, -x)$$

Clockwise Rotations
- 90°   : $$(x, y) \rightarrow (y,  -x)$$
- 180°  : $$(x, y) \rightarrow (-x, -y)$$
- 270°  : $$(x, y) \rightarrow (-y, x)$$

(Either one of these lists is easily derived from the other.)
